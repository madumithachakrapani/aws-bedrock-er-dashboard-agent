# ER Operational Efficiency – Knowledge Base

## Key Efficiency Insights

- **Wait Time ↑ → Satisfaction ↓**: There is a clear inverse relationship between wait time and patient satisfaction.
- **Weekdays** have higher visit volume AND higher wait times.
- **Weekends** have lower volume but similar wait times, indicating an **efficiency gap** — fewer patients but proportionally similar delays.
- **Peak congestion** occurs from **12 PM – 6 PM** across all days.
- **71–80 age group** reports the highest satisfaction (6.38); younger adult groups report the lowest.
- **Admitted patients are more satisfied** (+1.17 satisfaction score vs. non-admitted).
- General Practice is a system-wide bottleneck; Cardiology has lower satisfaction relative to its volume.
