# ER Department Performance – Knowledge Base

## Department Visit Share and Wait Times

| Department | Visit Share | Avg Wait Time |
|---|---|---|
| General Practice | 48.00% | 39.71 minutes |
| Gastroenterology | 10.87% | 35.77 minutes |
| Orthopedics | 16.29% | 35.38 minutes |
| Physiotherapy | 6.96% | 35.84 minutes |
| Cardiology | 7.30% | 37.61 minutes |
| Neurology | 5.77% | 36.12 minutes |
| Renal | 4.80% | 34.80 minutes |

### Key Observations
- **General Practice** is the busiest department, handling nearly half of all visits with the highest average wait time. It is the primary bottleneck in ER operations.
- **Renal** has the lowest wait time (34.80 min) and the lowest visit volume (4.80%).
- **Orthopedics** is the second largest by visit share at 16.29%.
- All departments exceed the 30-minute wait target, confirming a system-wide capacity issue.
