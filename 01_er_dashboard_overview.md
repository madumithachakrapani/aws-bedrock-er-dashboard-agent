# ABC Emergency Room (ER) Dashboard – Knowledge Base Overview

## Dashboard Summary

The ABC Emergency Room Dashboard covers the period **October 1, 2022 – August 31, 2024**. It is organized into three tabs:
1. **ER Overview** – High-level KPIs, visit trends, department performance, and heatmap
2. **Operational Efficiency** – Wait time vs. satisfaction, weekday/weekend breakdown, age cohort analysis
3. **Patient Demography** – Race and gender breakdowns of volume, wait time, satisfaction, and admission

## Operational Bottlenecks

- General Practice handles 48% of visits — highest load + wait time (~39.7 minutes)
- Peak delays observed in January–February (78%+ delayed visits)
- Consistent delay pattern indicates capacity constraints, not one-time spikes

---

## Peak Load Insights

- Highest patient volume during **12 PM – 5 PM**
- Mid-week (Tuesday–Thursday) shows heavier congestion
- Early morning and late night are underutilized capacity windows
